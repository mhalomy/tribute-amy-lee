Amy Lynn Lee Hartzler is an American singer, songwriter, pianist, and record producer. She is the co-founder and lead vocalist of the rock band Evanescence. She possesses a mezzo-soprano vocal range.   • 1987 - her three year old sister died from an unknown illness, to whom she wrote the songs "Hello" and "Like You" for
  • 1995 - Evanescence was formed with Ben Moody as her co-founder and guitarist
  • 1998-1999 - recorded the Evanescence EP and Sound Asleep EP respectively and sold them at local venues
